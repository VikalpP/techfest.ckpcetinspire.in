INDIAN ENTREPRENEUR(START UP INDIA)

“ASPIRE, BELIEVE, CREATE!!!”

Description

This event tries to bring out the hidden entrepreneur and helps them to showcase their talent. This event helps in building confidence & makes you believe that you’ve got the guts to be an entrepreneur.

Rules and Regulations :

  • This is an event in which an individual or a group of 2 can take part.
  • Participants have to present their idea in form of presentation/model(model is not mandatory).
  • The participants won’t be given any time to make the presentation on the day of the event so the participants should be ready with the presentation (ppt format) on the day of the event.(Prepare the presentation from home)
  • The judgement criteria for getting into the 2nd round will be based on
    • Feasibility
    • Cost-effectiveness
    • Time Limit etc depending upon the judge.
  • The participants will be allowed to present their idea within a time limit of maximum 10 minutes.
  • If we find any content that has copied/taken directly from the internet or social media , that particular participant/team will be directly disqualified.
  • NOTE: Number of rounds may be increased as per the number of participants.
