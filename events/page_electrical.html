Code Fest

Let’s crack the code.

Description

Code Fest is the coding festival organized by the Department of Electrical Engineering, CKPCET. Code Fest is a unique fest wherein concepts of Mathematics, logic, algorithms, language syntax, etc. are required to be deployed in programming; these concepts manifest themselves in solving problems effectively and efficiently!

Round 1 :

  1. This round consists of test which will be taken on paper.
  2. This round will have MCQ’s.
  3. Marks and Time will be take into account.
  4. Scrutiny Criteria will be decided by the jury.

Round 2 :

  1. This round consists of test which will be taken on paper.
  2. This round will have questions (no option will be provided) where participant have to write.
  3. Marks and Time will be take into account.
  4. Scrutiny Criteria will be decided by the jury.

Final :

  1. This round consists of coding which is conducted in computer lab.
  2. Editors to be used is Turbo C/C++.

Rules and Regulations :

  1. Organizer has the rights to change any rules or evaluation criteria, if necessary.
  2. Each participant must have the identity card of his/her respected institute.
  3. Decision taken by the judges will be consider as a final decision, and it cannot be challenged in any circumstances.
  4. Once the quiz starts, late entries will be disqualified.
  5. A participant must reach the location of the competition before 15 minutes else he/she will be disqualified.
  6. Use of Cell phones or Internet is prohibited.
  7. If any misbehaviour or indiscipline shown by the participants then they will be disqualified from the event at that time.
  8. In case of tie in final round Case-1: Overall performance (Marks) of 2 rounds and less time taken by candidate will be counted. Case-2: If still tie occurs, then one more round may be decided by the organizers

Circuitrix

Electrify the Brain.

Description

The event “Circuitrix” is based on the brain mapping of participants in the field of basic electrical and electronics engineering. The event involves theoretical as well as practical aspects based challenges. Different round for the event are as follows:

Round 1 :

  • This round is based on the basic fundamentals related to basic electrical and electronics engineering.

Round 2 :

  • Different challenging tasks will be given to participants related to basic electrical engineering, basic electronics and electrical machines. Every challenges will be time bounded in this round.

Round 3 :

  • Different challenging tasks will be given to participants related to basic electrical engineering, basic electronics and electrical machines. Every challenges will be time bounded in this round.

Final :

  • This round is based on real implementation of assigned task within given time limit. Necessary hints and components will be provided at the time of competition.

Rules and Regulations :

  1. Each group consist of 2 participants.
  2. All the necessary components will be provided.
  3. Students should have the knowledge of  basic electrical and electronics engineering.
  4. Event consists of three rounds.
  5. Disqualification rules :
    1. Any team not present at the allotted time will be disqualified by default.
    2. Use of mobile phone and Internet during the competition is strictly prohibited, if found that team will be directly disqualified at that moment.
    3. The decision of the organizers in declaring the results will be final. No queries in this regard in this regard will be entertained.
    4. In case of a conflict over a certain answer, the decision of coordinators will be final.
    5. Any member of a team found indulging in unacceptable behaviour will attract disqualification for the entire team.

Laser Maze

Beam bending logic.

Description

Laser Maze is to navigate the lasers like beam of light while being surrounded by a web of laser beams.
You must navigate carefully to cross different hurdles to achieve the high score and complete your mission.

Rules and Regulations:

  1. Participants from any branch or stream can participate in this event.
  2. Three participants are allowed in each team. Participants must register as a team.
  3. Participants must not possess any mirror or any other reflection friendly smooth materials during the event.
  4. No use of internet (via PC or Mobiles) is allowed during the event.
  5. On-time availability of participants is must. Schedule would be given in advance to every registered participant.
  6. Decision taken by jury will be considered as “FINAL”.
  7. No material except pen or pencil is needed from participant during event.
  8. Number of rounds in the event will be decided based on the entries and will be informed on the day of event.
