Corpo Trade

“ Price it right, it sells overnight, Price it wrong, it stays too long”

Description

Corpo trade is an event which brings out a trader who can utilize his/her potential to gain maximum earnings and enhances the capability to take right decisions at the right time.

Rules & Regulations:

ROUND 1 :

  • In this round a number of questions will be given one by one.
  • The participants have to give their response to the questions.
  • For each correct answer the team will be awarded 10 points and will be added cumulatively.
  • But for every wrong answer the scoreline would go directly to zero. So for eg: if a participant has given 3 correct responses and gives the 4th response wrong the score line would go back to zero.
  • Here the lifeline of “vault” will be given to the participants where they can store their scores .The scores once stored will be considered static and no deduction will be done from the stored scores. But these lifeline can be used only once. For eg: If a participant feels that the first 3 responses are correct and he is not sure about the 4th one ,he can use this lifeline so that score will be added to the vault but and there after the same rules for the next questions.
  • Further rules will be described to you at the time of the event.

ROUND 2 :

  • In this round the teams have to generate the items by selling the items but the details of this round will be given at the time of the event.

Recruit-E-Race (Honchos Frame)

“It’s all about you”

Description

The main objective of this event is to provide corporate experience of recruitment procedure so that you can enhance your potentialities and capabilities for grabbing new opportunities

Rules & Regulations :

ROUND 1 : (Written Test)

  • There will be 30 questions.
  • The time given will be 45 minutes.
  • Each question will have weightage of 1 mark.
  • There will be no negative marking.
  • The paper comprises of :
    • General English
    • Logical Reasoning
    • General Knowledge and
    • Current Affairs

ROUND 2 : (Youth Parliament / Group Discussion)

  • The main objective is to check how you behave, participate and contribute in a group, how much importance do you give to group objective as well as your own, how well do you listen to view-points of others and how open-mined are you in accepting views contrary to your own.
  • There will be maximum 8 members in one group.
  • Time allotment of 5 minutes will be given after delivering the topic to be discussed
  • Conclusion should be arrived within 15 minutes.
  • Slangs & abusing language is not allowed. If any critical situation arises, jury has the right to disqualified whole team.
  • No language barrier. Candidate can use any language on which he/she has a good command.

    • NOTE: Students are expected to reach before 15 minutes of stated time at predefined venue.

Skills to be judged:

  • How good you are at communication with others.
  • How you behave and interact with group.
  • How open mined are you.
  • Your listening skills.
  • How you put forward your views, delivering skills.
  • Your leadership and decision-making skills.
  • Your analysing skills and subject knowledge.
  • Problem solving and critical thinking skills.

Type : Solo

INDIAN ENTREPRENEUR (START UP INDIA)

“ASPIRE, BELIEVE, CREATE!!!”

Description

This event tries to bring out the hidden entrepreneur and helps them to showcase their talent. This event helps in building confidence & makes you believe that you’ve got the guts to be an entrepreneur.

Rules and Regulations :

  • This is an event in which an individual or a group of 2 can take part.
  • Participants have to present their idea in form of presentation/model(model is not mandatory).
  • The participants won’t be given any time to make the presentation on the day of the event so the participants should be ready with the presentation (ppt format) on the day of the event.(Prepare the presentation from home)
  • The judgment criteria for getting into the 2nd round will be based on
    • Feasibility
    • Cost-effectiveness
    • Time Limit etc depending upon the judge.
  • The participants will be allowed to present their idea within a time limit of maximum 10 minutes.
  • If we find any content that has copied/taken directly from the internet or social media , that particular participant/team will be directly disqualified.
  • NOTE: Number of rounds may be increased as per the number of participants.
