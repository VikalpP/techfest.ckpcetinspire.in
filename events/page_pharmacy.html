Pharma Recipe

If you have zeal to formulate uncommon, come and Prove!

Description

Formulate and dispense 30 grams of Cream

Pharma Models

Think Innovative, Be Smart.

Description

The event is to support the innovative and skilful idea of working and non-working pharma models which will be beneficial to the industry and society. It’s a platform for the participant to exhibit their innovative working and non-working models which bring changes in the profession of pharmacy.

Rules & regulations:

  • Event is open for all students of D. Pharm., B. Pharm. and M. Pharm courses.
  • Presentation of model should be broadly in English or Hindi and time allotted for presentation is maximum 5 mins. There will be negative marking if the time limit is exceeded.
  • Participant has to send the title of topic, category: Working/nonworking, space required for model presentation, if any requirement like water supply, gas supply, electricity etc. and brief summary in not more than150 words at the given E mail i.d. pharmamodelckpipsr@gmail.com to the host institute on or before 28th February 2017.
  • Participants would be evaluated on the basis of selection of topic, originality, innovation, creativity, practical applicability and presentation skill and beneficial to the society.
  • The caution should be taken to refrain from displaying obscenity, violence, prejudice, negative message or any form of vulgarity will earn a disqualification from the event.
  • Judges decision will be final.

Cure People with your Magic.Reciprocate yourself and be the best.

Description
